This device contains two 4-bit binary counters.  Counter 1
has two clock inputs allowing bi-octal counting.  CLK1A   
controls Qa while CLK1B controls Qd,Qc, and Qb.  For full
binary counting on counter 1 you must connect Qa to CLKB.
The counter is reset to zero when /CLR is taken low.
