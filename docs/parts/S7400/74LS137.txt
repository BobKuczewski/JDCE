This device consists of a 3-line to 8-line demultiplexer
with latched inputs.  When the latch enable (/GL) is low,
the device acts as a decoder/demultiplexer, when the latch
enable goes high, the inputs are latched.  The output enables
G1 and /G2 control the output of the device independent of
the data inputs.
