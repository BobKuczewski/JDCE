This device contains two 4-bit decade counters.  Counter 1
has two clock inputs, CLKA controls only the Qa output and
CLKB controls the Qd,Qc, and Qb outputs.  Counter 1 is 
capable of bi-quinary counting.  To use counter 1 as a full
decade counter you must connect Q1a to CLKB.  The counters
are reset to zero when /CLR is taken low.
