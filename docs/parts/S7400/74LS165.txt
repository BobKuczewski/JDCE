This device contains an 8-bit parallel-load serial-out shift
register.  Complementary outputs are provided from bit 8 of
the register.

+-------+--------+-----+-----+-------++---------+-----+
| SH/LD | CLKINH | CLK | SER | A...H || /Qa /Qb |  Qh |
=======================================================
|   L   |    X   |  X  |  X  | a...b ||  a   b  |  h  |
|   H   |    L   |  L  |  X  |   X   || Qa* Qb* | Qh* |
|   H   |    L   |  /  |  H  |   X   ||  H  Qa* | Qg* |
|   H   |    L   |  /  |  L  |   X   ||  L  Qa* | Qg* |
|   H   |    H   |  X  |  X  |   X   || Qa* Qb* | Qh* |
+-------+--------+-----+-----+-------++---------+-----+
* Previous state of Qn
